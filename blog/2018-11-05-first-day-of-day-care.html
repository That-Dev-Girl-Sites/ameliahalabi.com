First day of day care

November 5, 2018

Today was my first day of day care. I only spent a few hours at day care today, just to get used to it, but it was an exciting first day!

I met all the teachers and other babies. I ate. I napped. I played with ice cubes. I cried a little. (It’s so noisy there!) Mommy cried a little. (She missed me.) But we’ll both get used to it.
